MeloPost User Guide

Comprehensive manual for incident postmortems and systematic root cause analysis.

1 Introduction

MeloPost is a specialized application designed to help organizations move beyond simple "blame games" and into structured, systematic root cause analysis. By using the Swiss Cheese Model, MeloPost helps teams identify not just who made a mistake, but what layers of defense failed and how to prevent similar issues in the future.

Figure 1: The MeloPost Dashboard provides a central hub for incident management.

2 Key Concepts

The Swiss Cheese Model

Defenses are likened to slices of Swiss cheese. Slices (Layers) represent defenses. Holes represent weaknesses. Incidents occur when holes in every slice align.

Layers, Holes & Stories

  • Layer: Category of defense (e.g., Run, Test).
  • Hole: A specific weakness found in a layer.
  • Story: A remedial task created to plug a hole.

3 Getting Started

Upon logging in, you are greeted with the Dashboard. Here you can see key metrics and recent postmortems. Use the search bar to find specific incidents using keywords or tags.

Figure 2: Access the platform via the secure login screen.

4 Managing Postmortems

View and Filter

The "All Postmortems" view provides a filterable list of every incident in the system.

Creating a Postmortem

Click "Create New Postmortem" to begin. You'll need to provide basic incident data, including title, date, type, and references.

Impact & Evidence

Document the full impact and attach relevant documents like logs or screenshots.

Defense Layers

Analyze the 10 standard defense layers to identify where the systemic failures occurred.

Postmortem Notes

Capture raw data and detailed findings using the dedicated Notes section with Froala Editor V5.

Incident Notes

Capture raw data and detailed findings during a incident the dedicated Notes section with Froala Editor V5. These can at a later time be linked to a postmortem.

5 Swiss Cheese Analysis

Stories (Remedial Actions)

Link identified holes to actionable tasks (Stories) and track them through to completion.

Hole Overview

The Hole Overview provides a centralized view of all identified weaknesses across all postmortems.

Centralized Tracking

Filter and analyze holes by keyword, team, layer, or status to identify recurring systemic failures.
